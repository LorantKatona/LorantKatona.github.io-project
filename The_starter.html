The choice of the flour is individually variable. You can use either all-purpose white and hard type, whole grain, or mixture of these.

Method

To begin wit hour starter we mix thoroughly 20g of flour with 20ml of water. We let mixture to rest roughly 24 hours. On the second day we repect the very same step and let the mixture to rest another day. After the sixth day the starter is ready to be used, meaning that has enough and the right type of micbrobes to be able to fermentate a whole bread.
